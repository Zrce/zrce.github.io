MTU stands for Maximum Transmission Unit. It refers to the maximum size of a data packet that can be transmitted over a network in a single frame or packet. The MTU size is typically measured in bytes and is determined by the underlying network technology. It represents the largest amount of data that can be transmitted without fragmentation. Different network protocols and technologies may have different MTU sizes.
